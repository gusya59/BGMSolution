The main user is a representative of the company or business (small to medium) that deals with planning and calculating the budget for digital media marketing. In addition, our system provides a solution for marketers who wish to calculate the distribution of the budget for digital media advertising for their customers or to check the calculations made by them personally according to the latest information available in the system.

The system is a tool for streamlining the budget distribution process for digital media marketing. It allows you to save resources of time and money and to calculate an accurate division of the budget and the form of its allocation. The system will be a useful tool for marketing professionals who want to allocate accurate budget because the system takes into account all the characteristics of the customer's business and receives updated advertising prices for different advertising platforms.

In the future: The system will enable a thorough statistical analysis of the digital media marketing trends based on the data collected by the system. These data are designed to attract a new target audience of companies and entities interested in this information.
